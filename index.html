Main Character's in Sailor Moon

    Sailor Moon

    The main protaganist of the series and the leader of the Sailor Guardians. A careless 14 year old girl with an enormous capacity for love,compassion,and understanding.

    Sailor Mercury

    She is a quiet but intelligent 14 year old bookworm in Sailor Moon's class with a rumored IQ of 300.

    Sailor Venus

    She is a 14 year old perky dreamer, who appeared as a main protagonist in Codename: Sailor V.

  • character04

    Sailor Mars

    She is a elegant 14 year old shrine maiden. Because of her work as a Shinto priestess, Sailor Mars has limited precognition and can dispelor nullify evil using special scrolls.
